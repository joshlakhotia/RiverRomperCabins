Welcome to River Romper Residences in the heart of the Red River Gorge, Kentucky!

We offer a wide selection of cabins each designed to provide the perfect home away from home. Our cabins are located in the most beautiful and peaceful area of the Red River Gorge, offering our guests an unforgettable experience of the great outdoors. With amenities like hot tubs, fireplaces, and fully equipped kitchens, our cabins are perfect for a romantic getaway or a fun family vacation.

Come stay with us and experience the natural beauty and tranquility of the Red River Gorge!

How to get here

From Bert T Combs Mt. Pkwy, take exit 33

Head south on Hwy 11 for 14.5 miles

Turn right onto Hwy 1036 and continue for 5 miles

Take a slight left onto the dirt road with the "Mountain Laurel" sign.

Drive down the dirt road until you reach a fork, take a left.

You made it! Look for the "River Romper Residences" sign
